Entertainment Law

Legal counsel for musicians, producers, venues, and creative professionals

Home / Practice Areas / Entertainment Law

I represent music labels, producers, distributors, musicians, and other artists and industry professionals in entertainment law cases involving copyright law, contracts, trademarks and more. I am knowledgeable in all aspects of entertainment law.

Having spent decades as a performing artist and promoter, I know the music industry well. From licensing and contracts to IP protection, I support musicians, producers, venues, labels, and creatives of all types in managing their rights and relationships.

At Seattle Legal Advisor, I bring both legal expertise and real-world industry experience to entertainment law matters. Whether you're a solo artist negotiating your first record deal, a venue booking touring acts, or a label building your roster, I provide practical counsel that protects your interests and advances your career.

Industry-Informed Legal Counsel

Entertainment law isn't just about understanding contracts—it's about understanding the business. My background as a performer and promoter gives me insight into what really matters to artists and industry professionals.

Intellectual Property Protection

  • Fair Use Analysis: Understanding permissible uses of existing works
