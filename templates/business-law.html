Business Law

Strategic legal counsel for formation, contracts, M&A, and growth

Home / Practice Areas / Business Law

Whether you're launching a new venture, negotiating a deal, or navigating ownership transitions, I provide clear, strategic legal advice tailored to business owners and entrepreneurs in Seattle and throughout Washington State.

At Seattle Legal Advisor, I bring firsthand experience as a business owner, corporate counsel, and transactional attorney. I help clients choose the right entity structure, draft and negotiate contracts, protect intellectual property, and manage complex transactions like mergers, acquisitions, and succession planning. My goal is to help you make confident decisions that reduce risk, support growth, and preserve long-term value.

I work with closely held companies, startups, nonprofits, creatives, and landlords—guiding them through the key legal steps that protect their interests and strengthen their operations.

Comprehensive Business Services

I handle a full range of business law services, including formation, financial investment counseling, contract review and development, breach of contract, shareholder disputes and more. For experienced advocacy for your business, contact me today.

Business Services

Business clients rely on me for help with:

  • Business Formation & Structuring: Choose the right entity, protect your assets, and set up the foundation for long-term success.
  • Buy-Sell Agreements & Succession Planning: Prepare for ownership changes with clear, enforceable agreements that reduce uncertainty.
  • Contract Drafting, Review & Negotiation: Ensure your agreements reflect your goals and protect your interests—no jargon, no gaps.
  • Mergers & Acquisitions: Navigate the full lifecycle of a deal with due diligence, documentation, and strategic counsel.
  • Governance & Compliance: Stay compliant with Washington business law and proactively manage risk as your business grows.
  • Real-World Advice for Landlords & Property Managers: As a property owner and manager myself, I help new landlords structure their operations legally and effectively.
  • Creative & IP-Focused Ventures: For artists, promoters, and entertainment businesses, I offer insight from both legal and industry experience.

Business Formation & Entity Selection

Choosing the right business structure is critical to your success. I help clients evaluate and establish:

  • Limited Liability Companies (LLCs)
  • Corporations (C-Corp and S-Corp)
  • Partnerships (General and Limited)
  • Professional entities (PLLC, PC)
  • Nonprofit organizations
  • Joint ventures and strategic alliances

Corporate Governance & Compliance

Maintaining proper corporate governance protects your business and personal assets. I assist with:

  • Operating agreements and bylaws
  • Shareholder and partnership agreements
  • Board resolutions and corporate minutes
  • Annual filings and compliance requirements
  • Corporate restructuring and reorganization
  • Securities law compliance

Business Transactions

  • Equipment financing and leasing
  • Distribution and supplier agreements

  • Investment and funding documentation

Dispute Resolution

A well-structured business with clear contracts is your first line of defense against legal disputes. But when disagreements do happen—whether between partners, with vendors, or around customer obligations—I help clients work through them with clarity and composure. I bring a thoughtful approach to negotiation that combines strong communication, active listening, and creative problem-solving. My focus is always on protecting your interests while maintaining the relationships and integrity your business depends on.
