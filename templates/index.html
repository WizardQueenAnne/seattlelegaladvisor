About Scott

Scott M. Haapala is an attorney, husband, father, gardener and artist. His corporate legal career began working at Baker & McKenzie where international energy, corporate finance, and business transactions consumed all his time. Motivated to make a greater contribution to the environment, Scott moved to the Environmental Integrity Project to focus on environmental enforcement of the Clean Air and Clean Water Acts.

Scott has a thorough understanding of Washington law and knows first-hand the trials and tribulations that come with being a business owner because he has owned and managed multiple businesses in addition to his law practice. It can be hard to start and grow a business, and he is here to help.
