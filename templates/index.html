Legal Services Rooted in Strategy and Clarity

My practice focuses on helping clients make smart, informed decisions across a comprehensive range of legal areas.

Contract Law

From contract drafting, amending, and negotiation to handling breach of contract disputes, I make sure your agreements are sound, clear, and enforceable.

Business Formation

Launching a new venture in Washington? I help clients choose the right structure, handle compliance, and build a solid legal foundation.

Corporate & Business Law

I advise startups, closely held companies, employee-owned entities, nonprofits, and growing businesses on corporate formation, restructuring, and day-to-day legal needs.

Mergers & Acquisitions

Whether you're buying or selling a business, I'll guide you through the transaction process with strategic counsel and careful due diligence.

Business Succession Planning

When it's time to pass the torch, I'm here to help you do it smoothly—with plans that protect your legacy and secure your business's future.

Intellectual Property

Your ideas matter. I help clients protect their creative assets—trademarks, copyrights, and trade secrets—with proactive legal protection.

Entertainment Law

Having spent decades as a performing artist and promoter, I know the music industry well. From licensing and contracts to IP protection, I support musicians, producers, venues, labels, and creatives.

Estate Planning

For individuals and families, I provide estate planning services including wills, trusts, and powers of attorney—so your future is protected.
