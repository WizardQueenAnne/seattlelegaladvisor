Contract Law

Clear, enforceable agreements that protect your business interests

Home / Practice Areas / Contract Law

Contracts are at the core of every business relationship—yet too often, they're filled with unclear language, missing terms, or unintended risk. Whether you're drafting a new agreement or reviewing one someone else sent you, I help you protect your interests and understand exactly what you're signing.

At Seattle Legal Advisor, I work with clients across Washington State to draft, negotiate, and enforce contracts that align with their goals. My approach is practical, thorough, and always focused on helping you move forward with confidence.

Contract Protection

For your business to succeed, it is essential that you are represented by a knowledgeable contract law attorney. A well-drafted agreement is your first and best protection against lawsuits, misunderstandings, and business disruption.

Contract Services

From startups and nonprofits to creatives and real estate ventures, I assist with a wide range of business agreements, including:

  • Service contracts and consulting agreements
  • Partnership and operating agreements
  • Employment and independent contractor agreements
  • Commercial lease agreements
  • Vendor and supplier agreements
  • Nondisclosure (NDA) and non-compete agreements
  • Purchase and sale agreements
  • Software licensing agreements
  • Distribution and sales agreements
  • Joint venture agreements

I'll walk you through the terms, flag red flags, and suggest revisions or negotiation points that protect your business in real-world scenarios—not just on paper.

Contract Drafting & Review

Too often, I see business owners deal with the consequences of vague or one-sided contracts. I help ensure your contracts:

  • Clearly define responsibilities, payment terms, and timelines
  • Address intellectual property, confidentiality, and non-solicitation
  • Plan for exit scenarios, disputes, and enforcement
  • Are enforceable under Washington law and fit your specific use case
  • Include proper termination and breach provisions
  • Protect against liability and limit risk exposure

Contract Negotiation

Contract negotiation is both an art and a science. I bring years of experience helping clients:

  • Understand the other party's position and motivations
  • Identify key negotiation points and priorities
  • Develop strategic approaches to difficult terms
  • Balance risk and reward in complex agreements
  • Achieve favorable outcomes while preserving relationships

Breach of Contract Disputes

When conflicts arise, I'm here to resolve them efficiently—but my goal is always to help you avoid them in the first place. If you're facing a breach of contract situation, I can help you:

  • Assess the strength of your legal position
  • Explore resolution options before litigation
  • Negotiate settlements that protect your interests
  • Pursue enforcement through the courts when necessary
  • Recover damages for breaches that harm your business

Industry-Specific Contracts

I have extensive experience with contracts across many industries, including:

  • Technology and Software: SaaS agreements, API licenses, development contracts
  • Real Estate: Purchase agreements, leases, property management contracts
  • Entertainment: Recording contracts, licensing deals, performance agreements
  • Construction: Contractor agreements, subcontractor contracts, material supply agreements
  • Healthcare: Professional services agreements, vendor contracts, compliance documents
  • Manufacturing: Supply agreements, distribution contracts, OEM agreements

Tailored Legal Support

Whether you're starting a new project, hiring a new team member, or growing through strategic partnerships, I offer legal support that adapts to your business. My contract services are always tailored to your needs and designed to grow with you.

Don't sign a contract you don't fully understand—or rely on a template that might not protect you. I help Washington clients create strong, clear contracts that support long-term success.
