Comprehensive Corporate Counsel

Your corporation needs more than just formation documents—it needs ongoing legal strategy that evolves with your business. I provide the comprehensive corporate law services that protect your interests and fuel your growth.

Corporate Formation & Structure

Choosing the right corporate structure is critical to your business success. I help clients establish and maintain:

  • C-Corporations: Traditional structure for businesses planning to scale, seek investment, or go public
  • S-Corporations: Pass-through taxation with corporate benefits for smaller, closely-held businesses
  • Professional Corporations (PCs): Specialized structures for licensed professionals
  • Benefit Corporations: Purpose-driven entities that balance profit with social/environmental impact
  • Close Corporations: Flexible structures for smaller groups of shareholders
  • Foreign Corporations: Registration and compliance for out-of-state entities doing business in Washington

  • Voting agreements and proxy arrangements
  • Corporate record keeping and maintenance
